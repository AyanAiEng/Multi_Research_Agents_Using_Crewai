#### Buying Journey Map

1. **Awareness**
   - **Touchpoints**: Social media ads, university career services, online forums, LinkedIn recommendations
2. **Consideration**
   - **Research**: Reading reviews, comparing features on product websites and review sites
3. **Decision**
   - **Trial**: Free trials or limited free versions to assess the quality of output
4. **Adoption**
   - **Optimization**: Using the tool to refine and update their resume over time
5. **Advocacy**
   - **Sharing**: Recommending the product to peers and colleagues through social media, job fairs, and university networks

#### Switching Costs

1. **Current Tools (Google Docs, Microsoft Word)**
   - **Cost of Transition**: Minimal initial cost as these tools are free
   - **Effort Required**: Learning how to use a new interface and features
2. **Competitor Products (CVBuilder, Resumonk)**
   - **Cost of Transition**: Subscription fees for premium plans
   - **Effort Required**: Comparing features, setting up accounts, and reworking existing content

By understanding these personas, their pain points, willingness to pay, and the buying journey, we can effectively tailor our marketing strategies, product offerings, and customer support to meet the needs of fresh graduates in different markets.
