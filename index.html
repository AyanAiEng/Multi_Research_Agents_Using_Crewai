Five specialized AI agents work sequentially, each building on the insights of the previous. The result is a comprehensive, data-driven business report.

Each agent conducts its own research — market sizing, competitor mapping, persona building, and more.

4
📋

The Crew

Meet Your Research Team

Each agent is a domain expert with 15+ years of simulated experience. They collaborate like a real consulting team — but work in minutes, not months.

📊

Market Research Specialist

Senior Analyst

Estimates TAM, SAM, and SOM with real numbers. Identifies growth trends, regulatory hurdles, and market timing using Porter's Five Forces framework.

Output: Market Research Report
🎯

Competitive Intelligence Analyst

Strategist

Maps 4-6 direct competitors with pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and funding data. Identifies market gaps and assesses competitive moats.

Output: Competitive Matrix
👥

Customer Insights Researcher

User Research Lead

Builds 3 detailed user personas with demographics, pain points, willingness to pay, and buying journeys. Ranks segments by attractiveness.

Output: Customer Personas
🚀

Product Strategy Advisor

Product Strategist

Defines MVP features via MoSCoW prioritization, pricing models, 3-phase roadmap, and go-to-market channels tied to real customer pain points.

Output: Product Strategy Doc
💼

Business Strategy Analyst

Financial Advisor

Delivers the final verdict: Go, Conditional Go, or No-Go. Includes 3-year revenue projections, unit economics, and top 5 risks with mitigations.

Output: Business Verdict
