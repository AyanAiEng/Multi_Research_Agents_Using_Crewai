#### Market Gaps Analysis

1. **Sustainability Focus**: While there is a growing trend towards sustainability in hiring practices, many existing solutions do not explicitly address eco-friendly features such as reducing paper usage and carbon footprint.

2. **Advanced Personalization Options**: Some users may desire more advanced personalization options beyond industry-specific templates. Features like real-time feedback on resume language or tailored job search recommendations could be valuable.

3. **Integration with Learning Platforms**: Integrating AI-powered resume builders with learning platforms to offer personalized career development advice and training programs would meet a gap in the market, especially for fresh graduates looking to enhance their skills beyond just creating a resume.

4. **Zety**:

   - **Defensibility**: High, with strong brand loyalty and large user base.

5. **Resume.io**:

   - **Weaknesses**: Limited AI features compared to premium players like Resumonk.
